Popular Deductions You Should Check

💰

Section 80C

Claim deductions for ELSS funds, PPF, EPF contributions, life insurance premiums, home loan principal repayment, and tuition fees.

🏥

Section 80D

Reduce taxable income using health insurance premiums paid for yourself, family, and parents. Senior citizen benefits may increase savings.

❤️

Section 80G

Eligible donations to approved institutions can help lower tax liability while supporting meaningful causes.

🏠

Home Loan Benefits

Claim principal repayment under 80C and interest benefits under applicable housing loan provisions.

📈

Capital Gain Planning

Verify equity, mutual fund and property gains carefully. Incorrect reporting often delays processing.

📄

AIS Cross Check

Always compare your Form 26AS, AIS and TIS before filing to avoid mismatch notices later.

Before Filing, Verify
